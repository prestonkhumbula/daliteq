Is your Antivirus worth it?

Jan 15, 2022By Preston Khumbula05 Comments

Today, it is unquestionable that internet has made it to the basic needs list and it is quite true that the internet is an open ground as well for several cyber-criminals to dispose viruses. This will further add to the list another essential needs which happens to be an Antivirus software. An unprotected computer is like an open door for computer viruses, it can be a matter of seconds to open a website or a file and the next time your screen turns white. This will futher warranty the need for a good and efficient Antivirus software to keep you protected both online and offline. Well, folks, today let's go through a couple of features that your Antivirus software must possess.

  1. Real-time Scanning
    2. Antivirus software is specifically designed to detect the presence of malicious objects, sadly, not all of them detect them as they attempt to enter. Ineffective products require you to run some daily scan routines to see if your system is safe. Effective Antiviruses are always alert and keep track of every activity, plugging in any storage media like a flash drive will instantly trigger a scan service and feedback is echoed.It will further contact some background checks to make sure you are fully protected. In this case, ESET and Kasperky have proven their worthiness.
  2. Multiple App Protection
    3. Threats exist accross the entire computing scope. It can be emails, browsers and even games, your Antivirus package should be able to protect you from attacks coming from any angle. I really love the Kasperky browser extension.
  3. Frequent Automatic Updates
    4. The relevence of your Antivirus lies on its ability to update for newer versions of viruses and threats are released daily. Failure to do so simply mean that your protection is out of date and blind to all new threats that will enter undeterred.
  4. Protection From All Types of Threats
    5. Today's cyber-criminals are creative, they barely dispose viruses without using an attractive package. Some can be Trojans, Spyware and Adware; your Antivirus must be competent enough to protect you from all that.

That also means the antivirus software you are running on your own computer is likely only catching 5 percent of the emerging threats targeting your machine. If your body's own immune system had a batting average like that, you would be dead in a matter of hours

- Marlon Bikinesi
